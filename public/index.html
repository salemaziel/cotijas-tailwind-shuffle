Welcome to Cotijas Mexican Grill and Seafood

As a family owned restaurant in San Diego, Cotijas offers a dynamic and authentic dining experience for lovers of Mexican cuisine. Our menu features a wide selection of traditional Mexican dishes, including tacos, burritos, fajitas, and enchiladas, as well as fresh seafood dishes such as ceviche, fish tacos, and shrimp cocktails. Each dish is prepared using only the freshest ingredients, sourced from local suppliers whenever possible.
